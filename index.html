A WvW Guide for Firebrand

Gear
For the healing firebrand build, you ideally want full minstrel, if you can't afford that go with Nomad armor and Minstrel Trinkets and Minstrel Weapons.
While it is expensive to obtain if crafting, it is the best for what a support Firebrand needs. You want the boon duration and the healing power, as well as the toughness and vitality. For the Infusions you want the Healing infusions, put in a few Vitality ones if you feel you need it.

Runes & Sigils
For Runes: Rune of the Monk is your ideal choice. If you don't have that, Water or Mercy are cheap alternatives, Defender runes for the beginner Firebrand.

For Sigils: You want Transference/Benevolence in your staff, and Transference/Energy in your Mace/Shield.

Skill Bar

Healing Skill: Mantra of Solace was chosen because its 3 heals, and it provides Aegis.

Utility Skills: Stand your Ground, is the standard for Stability. Mantra of Lore is your condition cleanse. Merciful Intervention, is a great utility skill but does come with risks. It can be used to fast rez downs. But use it carefully, do not risk your own safety for that of a squadmate. It can also be used to snap yourself back into position if you find yourself where you shouldn't.

Elite Skills: Mantra of Lore is a second source of stability, as well as 3 stun breaks.

Specs

Use the build shown above.

Honor: Invigorated Bulwark to help with outgoing healing, Pure of heart, for additional self healing, and Force of Will for additional vitality.
The passives also really aid in the support roll.

Valor: Smiter's Boon for condi clear on heal, strength in numbers for more toughness for your subgroup, and Tenacious Defense for lowering Tome of Courage CD on blocks.

Firebrand: How everything gets tied together. Archivist of Whispers: Additional tome pages. Stalwart Speed: Anytime you apply Aegis or Stability you apply quickness. This ensures anytime you give your party boons you also apply quickness; which helps with group dps. Loremaster: For lower cooldowns on tomes.

Consumables

Foods: Oysters with Pesto Sauce, gives you damage reduction and healing. Excellent for the frontline healing role that is support firebrand.

Oils: Bountiful Maintenance Oil, is more outgoing healing power.

For general tome usage, DO NOT spam your 1 skill, you want to save your tome charges for the skills you need.
This allows you to rotate your cooldowns, and hopefully your tomes will be off cd by the time you've used another up.
Firebrands should do their best to be vocal with their calls, if you do not own a mic, you should listen to your other guard calls and alternate your tomes accordingly.
